## Introduction[<span style="margin-left: 10px; text-decoration: none;">📌</span>](#introduction)

Tired of having to learn a massive amount of coursework and struggling to find a way to automatically summarize it? You're in the right place!

This project aims to summarize courses, in PDF format, using a machine learning model (LLM), [Mistral](https://mistral.ai).

Unlike existing solutions, the process is optimized to summarize courses efficiently and quickly.

## Why This Project[<span style="margin-left: 10px; text-decoration: none;">🤔</span>](#why-this-project)

This project originated from a request by a medical student who was falling behind and wanted to summarize his courses quickly. He initially tried using ChatGPT, but it wasn’t precise enough because the courses were far too lengthy.

That's why I decided to create an optimized solution to ensure maximum accuracy, regardless of the number of pages in the coursework.
